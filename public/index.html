The Home of the CIL

The Casualness Intensifies League, or the CIL is a competitive singles, draft-style Pokemon league with a unique, casual approach. Don't get it twisted--coaches in the CIL play and prep at a very high level and often win other leagues in which they compete. The laid-back feel comes from an honest approach and love of the game that draws in coaches who want to have fun, learn, and play the game to be the very best, like no one ever was.
