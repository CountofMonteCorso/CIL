Experience the CIL

Welcome to the CIL, the Casualness Intensifies League. We are a competitive singles, draft-style Pokemon league with a unique, casual approach. Don't get it twisted--coaches in the CIL play and prep at a very high level and often win other leagues in which they compete, but we welcome players of all experience-levels as long as they have a good attitude and willingness to learn. The laid-back feel comes from an honest approach and love of the game that draws in coaches who want to have fun, learn, and play the game to be the very best, like no one ever was. Explore the site for battle results, rosters, coach profiles and more that will take your intensity to the next level.
